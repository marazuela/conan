## New Entries by Month

### July 2026

**ORCA-T** (Orca Bio) — BLA — Jul 6
- Indication: AML, ALL, MDS (hematologic malignancies)
- Allogeneic T-cell immunotherapy from matched donors
- Priority Review. Extended from Apr to Jul due to CMC Major Amendment (not safety/efficacy)

- **Note**: Private company — no public ticker. Not actionable for our pipeline but useful context.

### August 2026 (9 entries — busiest month)

**BMY — iberdomide** — NDA — Aug 17
- Indication: Relapsed/refractory multiple myeloma (IberDd combo)
- Priority Review + Breakthrough Therapy
- Phase 3 EXCALIBER-RRMM (NCT04975997): significant MRD negativity improvement
- New drug class: cereblon E3 ligase modulator (CELMoD)
- **Investability**: Mega-cap ($130B+). No edge for our pipeline — widely followed.

**BRTX — adrabetadex** — NDA — Aug 17
- Indication: Niemann-Pick disease type C
- Orphan drug. No approved treatments exist.
- **Investability**: Small/micro-cap. May be interesting if signal converges.

**SVRA — molbreevi** — BLA — Aug 22
- Indication: Autoimmune pulmonary alveolar proteinosis (PAP)
- Priority Review. Orphan drug. First-and-only potential approved therapy.
- EMA MAA also validated. MHRA accepted in UK.
- **Investability**: Small-cap ($500M–$1B range). First-in-class for rare disease. Worth watching.

**CAPR — deramiocel** — BLA — Aug 22
- Indication: DMD cardiomyopathy
- Class 2 resubmission after CRL. Priority Review.
- HOPE-3 Phase 3 met primary (PUL v2.0) and key secondary (LVEF) endpoints
- First-in-class cell therapy for DMD. PRV eligible.
- **Investability**: Small-cap. Resubmission after CRL adds risk but data is strong. Binary catalyst.

**IONS — zilganersen** — NDA — Aug 22
- Indication: Alexander disease (ultra-rare neurodegenerative)
- Orphan drug. Second IONS PDUFA in 2026 after olezarsen (Jun 30)
- **Investability**: Large-cap ($12.6B). Second catalyst in 2 months — watch for compound effect.

**RARE — DTX401** — BLA — Aug 23
- Indication: Glycogen storage disease type Ia (GSDIa)
- Priority Review. AAV gene therapy. Multiple designations (Orphan, Fast Track, RMAT, Rare Pediatric Disease).
- GlucoGene Phase 3 met endpoint. US manufacturing at Bedford, MA facility.
- **Investability**: Mid-cap (~$3–4B). Gene therapy pioneer. Two PDUFAs (Aug 23, Sep 19). Very interesting.

**XSPRAY.ST — Dasynoc** — NDA — Aug 25
- Indication: CML and ALL (improved dasatinib formulation)
- 505(b)(2) resubmission after CRL. HyNap platform technology.
- **Investability**: Swedish-listed. Small-cap. Reformulation play — limited upside vs pure novel drugs.

**PHAR — Besremi** — sBLA — Aug 30
- Indication: Essential thrombocythemia (label expansion from polycythemia vera)
- Orphan drug. Already marketed for PV.
- **Investability**: Small/mid-cap. Label expansion — moderate catalyst.
