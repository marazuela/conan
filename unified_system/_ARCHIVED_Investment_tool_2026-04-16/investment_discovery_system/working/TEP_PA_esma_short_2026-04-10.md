## Thesis (from Market Narrative)

The short thesis is that Teleperformance, as a business process outsourcing (BPO) and customer experience (CX) services leader, faces existential risk from generative AI. The argument: AI-powered chatbots, voice agents, and LLMs will automate the front-line customer support work that generates the bulk of TP's ~$10B+ annual revenue. Shorts expect revenue growth to decelerate and margins to compress as large enterprise customers insource AI automation.

## Counter-Thesis (Contrarian Long)

There are credible arguments the AI threat is overstated:
1. **Volume arithmetic**: Enterprise AI chatbots already handle ~95% of service volume at the first level. CX firms like TP already only handle the ~2-5% of calls that AI failed on — the complex, nuanced, escalated, or empathetic ones. The easy volume is already automated.
2. **Historical precedent**: TP has weathered prior tech shifts (offshoring, IVR, chatbots v1) and remained the category leader.
3. **Future Forward plan**: TP's own strategic plan (June 2025) targets 4–6% annual organic growth through 2028 with a 15.5% EBITA margin, incorporating AI investments.
4. **Valuation**: After the decline, TP trades at compressed multiples. If the AI thesis softens, multiple expansion could drive significant upside.
