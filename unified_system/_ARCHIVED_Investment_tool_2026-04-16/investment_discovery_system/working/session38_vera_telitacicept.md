# VERA Competitive Intel: Telitacicept (RemeGen) — S38

## Question
Is telitacicept an imminent US competitive threat to atacicept for IgAN?

## Finding: NO — medium-term risk only

**Telitacicept (RemeGen/Vor Bio):**
- Same mechanism class as atacicept: dual BAFF+APRIL inhibitor
- Phase 3 data in China (318 patients): -55-59% UPCR reduction at 39 weeks (impressive, comparable to atacicept ORIGIN)
- China BLA submitted for IgAN (would be 5th approved indication in China for telitacicept)
- US: FDA approved IND for US Phase 3 trial (Nov 2022). Trial is ongoing but NO US NDA filing imminent.

**Timeline assessment:**
- China approval: Possible H2 2026
- US Phase 3 completion: ~2027 at earliest
- US NDA filing: ~2028
- US approval (if successful): 2029+

**Impact on VERA thesis:**
- Telitacicept is NOT a near-term (~12 month) competitive threat in the US market
- Atacicept, if approved July 2026, would have at minimum 2-3 years of US exclusivity in the dual BAFF+APRIL class
- The existence of telitacicept's strong data actually VALIDATES the mechanism — which is mildly positive for atacicept's approvability
- Long-term (2029+), telitacicept could be a same-class competitor, but by then atacicept will have established market position

**Score impact:** None. This risk was already implicitly discounted in the Info Asymmetry dimension. No score change needed.
