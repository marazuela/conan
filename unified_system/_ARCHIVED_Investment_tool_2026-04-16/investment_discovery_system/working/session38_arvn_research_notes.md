# ARVN Pre-Research Notes — S38

**Purpose:** Background research ahead of planned T-20 deep dive (~May 15, 2026)
**Current score:** ~22.5 (watchlist)

## Key Facts

- **Drug:** Vepdegestrant (ARV-471) — oral PROTAC ER degrader
- **Indication:** ESR1-mutated ER+/HER2- advanced/metastatic breast cancer, 2L+ monotherapy
- **Mechanism:** First oral PROTAC protein degrader to reach NDA (novel mechanism class)
- **Partner:** Pfizer (50/50 collaboration)
- **Market cap:** ~$658M @ $10.82 (Apr 10)
- **Analyst consensus:** Buy, avg PT $14-18, high $51, low $6

## Clinical Evidence
- **VERITAC-2 (Phase 3):** Global, randomized vs fulvestrant. Published NEJM + presented ASCO 2025.
  - PFS (ESR1m): 5.0 mo vs 2.1 mo (HR 0.57, 43% risk reduction) — statistically significant
  - Blinded independent central review (BICR) confirmed

## Regulatory
- NDA accepted by FDA
- PDUFA June 5, 2026
- No Priority Review mentioned — standard review timeline
- No Breakthrough Therapy mentioned in search results

## Investment Considerations

**Bull case:**
- First PROTAC approval = platform validation (massive for Arvinas's pipeline value)
- ESR1m breast cancer has high unmet need; fulvestrant is weak comparator
- Pfizer commercial infrastructure for launch
- $10.82 stock price seems to price in significant risk — upside on approval could be substantial

**Bear case:**
- $658M mcap — small cap, volatile
- PFS of 5.0 months is modest in absolute terms (though comparator was weak)
- ESR1m is a narrower indication than all-comer ER+ — addressable market may be limited
- No Priority Review or BTD suggests FDA may have questions
- Multiple analyst PTs near current price suggests limited upside already modeled
- Pfizer collaboration economics — ARVN gets 50% of US profits but also bears 50% of costs
