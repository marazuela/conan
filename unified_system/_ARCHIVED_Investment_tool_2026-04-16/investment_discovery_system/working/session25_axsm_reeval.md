## Key facts (verified)

1. **4 Phase 3 trials total**:
   - ADVANCE-1: POSITIVE (statistically significant on CMAI)
   - ACCORD-1: POSITIVE
   - ACCORD-2: POSITIVE (presented at AAN 2025)
   - ADVANCE-2: **MISSED** primary endpoint (CMAI 13.8 vs 12.6 placebo, 408 pts) — numerical improvement only

   **Hit rate: 3 of 4 = 75%**. The FDA regularly approves on 2-of-2 or 2-of-3 stat-sig hits. 3-of-4 with a controlled safety database and Priority Review is a meaningfully positive profile, not "mixed."

2. **Priority Review granted** — FDA only grants Priority Review where there is unmet need AND the initial data package supports a potentially favorable benefit-risk assessment. Priority Review is a positive directional signal in itself.

3. **Safety profile well-characterized** — AXS-05 is already approved as Auvelity for MDD (2022); the safety database from commercial use is large. FDA emphasis on "no increased mortality, sedation, or cognitive decline" in trials is crucial for an ADA population where safety is the #1 concern (brexpiprazole AdCom was fractious on cognitive/cerebrovascular risk).

4. **Unmet need is HUGE**: ADA affects ~50% of AD patients. Only brexpiprazole (Rexulti, Otsuka/Lundbeck) is approved; it carries a boxed warning. A second approved option with a cleaner safety profile would address significant market demand.

5. **Company commercial prep is aggressive**:
   - **Doubling the Auvelity sales force from 300 to 600 reps** (effective 2026)
   - Stated this plan publicly at Oppenheimer Conference in February
   - Q1 earnings May 4 (post-PDUFA) — guidance will lock in ADA expectations
   - April 1 M&A: acquired balipodect (selective PDE10A inhibitor for schizophrenia) — management signaling confidence in pipeline expansion
   - This is not the behavior of a company worried about its PDUFA outcome.

6. **Street targets above current**:
   - Jefferies Buy $245 PT (+37%)
   - Mizuho Outperform $230 PT (+29%)
   - Current $178.90 vs $245 implies approved & ramping

7. **Auvelity MDD base business**: ~$600M annualized run-rate (per 2025 Q4 guide). This is a growing, real commercial asset — not a binary biotech. A CRL on ADA would hurt but not destroy AXSM; the MDD franchise floors the downside.
