## REVEAL-1 Data Interpretation

**Official headline (company framing):** POSITIVE — primary endpoint met
- Q4W elegrobart: 54% proptosis responder rate vs 18% placebo (placebo-adj 36%)
- Q8W elegrobart: 63% vs 18% (placebo-adj 45%)
- Diplopia resolution Q4W: 51% vs 16% placebo (+35%)
- Safety: tinnitus-based hearing events 11.3%/2.3% vs placebo
- BLA submission for SC formulation: Q1 2027

**Market reaction (why -43.8%):**
- Investor expectations for Q4W responder rate were 51-73% placebo-adjusted; delivered only 36-45%
- The comparator is Tepezza (teprotumumab, Amgen/Horizon): 83% responder rate in the original pivotal
- The Nov 2025 Amgen "kill" (Session 23) was the launch of Tepezza subcutaneous — competitive benchmark
- Elegrobart SC was supposed to be differentiated by being BETTER than Tepezza SC; REVEAL-1 shows it's not clearly better
- Without SC differentiation, the veligrotug IV label has a ~3-5 year franchise window before Tepezza SC erodes it

## Impact on Veligrotug (IV) PDUFA (June 30)

4. **Capital question** — VRDN cash runway is shorter without a clear franchise extension; may need raise
