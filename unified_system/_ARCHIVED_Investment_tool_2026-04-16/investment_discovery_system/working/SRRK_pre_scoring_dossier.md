## Why This Is Interesting

### 1. Manufacturing-Only CRL Pattern
The Sept 2025 CRL was **solely** related to observations at Catalent Indiana (now Novo Nordisk) fill-finish facility — a third-party CMO issue. The CRL:
- Did NOT cite clinical efficacy or safety concerns
- Did NOT require additional clinical trials
- Was not specific to apitegromab
- Resubmission was "limited in scope" — manufacturing update + standard safety update

**Historical pattern**: Manufacturing-only CRLs that are resolved quickly (6 months) have very high approval rates on resubmission (>90% based on industry data). The FDA's issue was with the facility, not the drug.
