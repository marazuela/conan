## Drug & Indication

- **Asset:** Atacicept
- **Mechanism:** B-cell modulator targeting both BAFF and APRIL (dual cytokine blocker)
- **Indication:** IgA nephropathy (IgAN) in adults
- **Regulatory path:** Accelerated Approval Program + Breakthrough Therapy Designation
- **Phase 3 trial:** ORIGIN (randomized, placebo-controlled)

## Pivotal Data (ORIGIN Phase 3 interim)

- **Primary endpoint met:** 46% reduction in proteinuria (24-hour UPCR) from baseline at week 36
- **Placebo-adjusted:** 42% reduction vs placebo, p<0.0001
- **Published:** New England Journal of Medicine (late 2025)
- **Presented:** ASN Kidney Week 2025
- **Clinical meaningfulness:** IgAN proteinuria reduction correlates with renal function preservation; FDA previously accepted proteinuria as surrogate for Filspari/sparsentan (TVTX) accelerated approval in 2023.

## Cross-Read to TVTX (HIGHLY RELEVANT)

**Critical observation:** Atacicept and sparsentan (TVTX's Filspari) both target IgA nephropathy but via completely different mechanisms:
- **Sparsentan (TVTX):** Dual endothelin/angiotensin receptor antagonist — hemodynamic approach
- **Atacicept (VERA):** B-cell/plasma cell modulator — immunologic approach targeting the upstream autoantibody production that drives IgAN

These are **complementary**, not competitive. Atacicept's approval doesn't directly cannibalize Filspari — in fact, combination therapy is plausible long-term. However, the commercial narrative matters: if atacicept shows superior proteinuria reduction (46% vs Filspari's ~45% in PROTECT), it becomes the "best-in-disease" claim that could reposition Filspari as a tier-2 therapy.

**Key insight for TVTX monitoring:** If TVTX's FSGS PDUFA (Apr 13) is approved, TVTX is already a 2-indication kidney franchise (IgAN + FSGS). A VERA approval in July creates competitive dynamics only in IgAN, which could cap TVTX upside but doesn't kill it. Conversely, if TVTX is CRL'd on FSGS, its IgAN franchise becomes the only revenue pillar just as VERA enters — this increases bear pressure.

**TVTX ↔ VERA read-across matters for position sizing, not binary kill conditions.**
